Calculator

Welcome to Calculator App

Features:

  • (+) Addition
  • (-) Substraction
  • (*) Multiplication
  • (/) Division

How to use:

Using interface:

  1. Input your first number by pressing the number buttons (0-9) on the interface
  2. Choose your operand by pressing the operator button (+, -, *, /) on the interface
  3. Input your second number by pressing the number buttons (0-9) on the interface
  4. Press the equals button (=) on the interface to get the results

Using keyboard:

  1. Input your first number by pressing the number buttons (0-9) on your keyboard
  2. Choose your operand by pressing the operator button (+, -, *, /) on your keyboard
  3. Input your second number by pressing the number buttons (0-9) on your keyboard
  4. Press the equals button (=) on your keyboard to get the results
